Post 1 - Homemade Slushie

Summer is here and that means its time for Slushies! Ditch your local Ice cream truck and follow this recipe instead for your very own Homemade Slushies! (Credits: https://www.seededatthetable.com/homemade-slushie-recipe/)

Ingredients

  • Club soda (or tonic water, or Sprite)
  • Sugar
  • Kool-aid powder
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Pour Club soda, sugar and Kool-aid powder into blender.
  2. Add ice and blend.

Post 2 - Places to Hike

Are you looking to hike this summer? I have hiked all over Toronto and here are the top 2 places I recommend!

  1. Scarborough Bluffs Park

    2. At roughly 6,400m long with minimal elevation gain and loss, it's the perfect place for beginners to try hiking as well as get an amazing view at Toronto's Great Lake!

  2. Rouge National Urban Park

    3. The Glen Eagles Vista Train at Rouge National Urban Park is an easy 600m Hike with amazing views of the rivers path. What's even better is that after the hike you can visit the Toronto Zoo or even Rouge beach which are located right next to Rouge National Urban Park!
