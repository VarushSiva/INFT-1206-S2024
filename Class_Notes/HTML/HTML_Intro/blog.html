Post 1 - Homemade Slushie

Summer is here and that means its time for Slushies! Ditch your local Ice cream truck and follow this recipe instead for your very own Homemade Slushies

Ingredients

  • Club soda (or tonic water, or Sprite)
  • Sugar
  • Kool-aid powder
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Pour Club soda, sugar and Kool-aid powder into blender.
  2. Add ice and blend.
